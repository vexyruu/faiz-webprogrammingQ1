Tahu Tek

Tahu Tek is a traditional dish from East Java, particularly from the city of Surabaya. Tahu Tek consists of half-fried tofu and lontong cut into small pieces using scissors. It also includes egg, fried potatoes, a bit of bean sprouts, and thinly sliced cucumbers. The dish is then topped with peanut sauce mixed with fermented shrimp paste (petis), and small prawn crackers sprinkled on top.
