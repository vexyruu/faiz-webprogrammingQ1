Tunjungan Street

Jalan Tunjungan is a historic and iconic street located in the heart of Surabaya, Indonesia. Known for its colonial-era buildings and vibrant atmosphere, the street has been a central part of Surabaya's cultural and commercial life for decades. It is home to landmarks like the famous Hotel Majapahit and features a blend of modern shopping centers, restaurants, and traditional markets. Jalan Tunjungan plays a key role in the city’s heritage, often associated with its role in Indonesia’s struggle for independence. The street is also a popular spot for tourists and locals alike.
